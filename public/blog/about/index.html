{* Adventurer, Writer, Coach *}

My main focus is to create a vibrant work environment where every member of a development team delights in their own contribution. As a seasoned veteran of high-transactional website development, I have a wealth of experience working with a variety of teams (agile and fragile). I combine lean and agile principles with leading edge technology and tidbits of philosophical wisdom to form an approach to software development that helps teams flourish. I love anything to do with the mysteries of outer space. I’m also a vegan who loves to cook, play a few chords on the guitar and sing off-key when no one’s listening. I have produced, directed and edited a number of little known documentary and art films.
